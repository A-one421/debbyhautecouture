Our Story

Crafting Dreams
Since 2015

A journey of passion, elegance, and unwavering commitment to making every woman feel extraordinary

The Beginning

Our Journey

DEBBY HAUTE COUTURE was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to make luxury fashion accessible without compromising on quality or elegance. Founded in 2015 by Deborah Adeyemi, our brand has grown from a small atelier in Lagos to one of Nigeria's most celebrated ready-to-wear couture houses.

Every piece we create tells a story of craftsmanship, attention to detail, and a deep understanding of the modern woman's needs. We believe that true elegance is timeless, and our collections reflect this philosophy through carefully curated designs that transcend seasonal trends.

Today, DEBBY HAUTE COUTURE serves thousands of women across Nigeria, helping them look and feel their absolute best for every occasion. Our commitment remains unchanged: to deliver exceptional quality, impeccable fit, and designs that make you feel truly glamorous.

What We Stand For

Our Values

Passion

Every stitch, every fabric choice, every design decision is driven by our unwavering passion for creating beautiful, wearable art that empowers women.

Excellence

Community

Our customers are family. We're committed to building lasting relationships and creating experiences that go beyond just selling clothes.

The Visionary

Meet Deborah

Deborah Adeyemi Semilore, founder and creative director of DEBBY HAUTE COUTURE, has always had an eye for elegance and a passion for empowering women through fashion. With over 15 years of experience in the fashion industry, she brings a unique blend of traditional craftsmanship and contemporary design sensibility to every collection.

"Fashion is more than just clothing—it's about confidence, self-expression, and feeling beautiful in your own skin," says Deborah. "Every piece we create at DEBBY is designed with this philosophy in mind."

Under her leadership, DEBBY HAUTE COUTURE has become synonymous with luxury, quality, and timeless elegance, dressing some of Nigeria's most influential women for their most important moments.
